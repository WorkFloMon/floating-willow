Blackberries grow wild along the hillsides of Marin County, just above the shimmering waters of Richardson Bay in Sausalito, California.

Each jar is made in small batches with fresh lemon and pure vanilla extract — no fillers, no artificial preservatives. Just the taste of the place where we live.
